Firebase Setup
One-time configuration — takes about 5 minutes
1
Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click Add project, name it anything (e.g. amt-quiz), then click through and hit Create project.
2
In your project, click Build in the left sidebar → Realtime Database → Create Database. Choose any location, then select Start in test mode and click Enable.
3
Click the gear icon ⚙ → Project settings. Scroll down to Your apps and click the </> Web icon. Give the app a nickname, click Register app, then copy the databaseURL value — it looks like https://amt-quiz-default-rtdb.firebaseio.com — and paste it below.
4
On that same page, copy the apiKey value (starts with AIza...) and paste it below.
5
Choose an Instructor Password that only you will know. Students will never see this.
